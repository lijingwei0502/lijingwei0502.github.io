About Me

I am now a first-year Ph.D student at Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS), Tsinghua University. I am very fortunate to have Professor Jingzhao Zhang as my advisor. Previously, I did my undergraduate at Department of Mathmatics and science, Tsinghua University.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in the theory of Reinforcement learning. I am also interested in the probabilistic theory and stochastic process, especially in Markov mixing time and its application in the graph theory.
